About me

Hi, I’m Chelsea! I grew up in Chongqing, China, and have spent the last seven years in California (five years in Santa Barbara and two in Orange County). My background bridges technology and the humanities, having a double degree in Statistics and English at UC Santa Barbara. From 2021 to 2023, I worked as a Product Analyst in an insure-tech startup, where I led data analysis to improve profitability and customer experience, building narratives with data to inform decision-making. Currently, I’m pursuing a Master’s in Information Management and Systems at UC Berkeley. I’m focused on exploring the intersection of information technology and human society, particularly in areas like algorithm fairness and ethical AI. In my free time, I enjoy photography, music (I'm learning guitar!), visiting museums, and watching sunsets by the beach.
