About me

Hi, I’m Chelsea! I grew up in Chongqing, China, and have spent the last seven years in California (five years in Santa Barbara and two in Orange County). My background bridges technology and the humanities, allowing me to approach information challenges from both technical and human-centered perspectives. Currently, I’m pursuing a Master’s in Information Management and Systems at UC Berkeley. I’m focused on exploring the intersection of information technology and human society, particularly in areas like algorithm fairness and ethical AI. In my free time, I enjoy photography, music (I'm learning guitar!), visiting museums, and watching sunsets by the beach.
